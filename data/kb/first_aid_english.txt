First Aid Guide (English):

1. Cuts and Wounds: Wash the wound with clean water. Apply pressure with a clean cloth to stop bleeding.
2. Burns: Hold the burned area under cool running water for 10-15 minutes. Do not apply oil or butter.
3. Diarrhea: Drink plenty of Oral Rehydration Salts (ORS) or fluids. Continue eating small, frequent meals.
4. Fever: Rest and drink plenty of fluids. You can take Paracetamol if the fever is above 102°F (38.9°C).

This information is for guidance only. In case of serious emergency, contact local medical services immediately.
